## Confessional Documents

**Book of Concord** = 《协同书》 (the whole collection, 1580)

| English | Chinese | Citation format |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Book of Concord | 《协同书》 | — |
| Augsburg Confession | 《奥斯堡信条》 | 奥斯堡信条，第N条：N |
| Apology of the Augsburg Confession | 《辩护书》 | 辩护书，第N条：N |
| Smalcald Articles | 《施马加登信条》 | 施马加登信条，第N部分，第N条：N |
| Formula of Concord | 《协和信条》/《协同式》 | 见下方说明 |
| Epitome (of Formula of Concord) | 《协和信条》摘要 | 协和信条 摘要，第N条：N |
| Solid Declaration (of Formula of Concord) | 宣言全文 | 协同式 宣言全文，第N条：N |
| Large Catechism | 《大问答》 | 大问答，第N条诫命：N |
| Small Catechism | 《小问答》 | 小问答，[部分]：N |
| Council of Trent | 特伦托公会议 | — |

**Formula of Concord note:** Lange uses 协同式 (informal), Pieper uses 协和信条 (standard). Within one book, pick one and be consistent. The Solid Declaration is always 宣言全文.

## Key Distinctions to Watch

| Term | Correct | Wrong / Confusing |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| Divine revelation | 启示 | 揭示（secular: uncover/reveal a secret） |
| actual sin | 本罪 | 本身实行罪（clumsy calque） |
| Book of Concord (collection) | 《协同书》 | 《协同式》（that's just the Formula） |
| Formula of Concord (one document) | 《协和信条》/《协同式》 | 《协同书》（that's the whole collection） |
| syncretism | 调和主义 | 协同主义（that's synergism） |
| Amyraldist (Reformed) | 阿米洛得主义者 | 亚米念主义者（different school entirely） |

## Scripture Citation Formats

- Chinese Bible: 《和合本修订版》(RCUV) — standard for BibleThought
- Format: （书卷章:节） e.g. （约3:16）（林前2:2）
- Use full-width brackets （）and half-width colon :
- Half-width colon INSIDE verse references: 约3:16 ✅, 约3：16 ❌

## Content Filtering Notes

Topics that may trigger content filtering in subagent outputs. Use neutral academic framing:

- "False teachings" → "comparative theological analysis" or "alternative positions"
- "Heresies" → "heterodox positions" or "departures from confessional teaching"
- Reprobation / eternal damnation → "the doctrine of divine judgment"
- Strong polemical language → soften to "Pieper critiques..." or "the Lutheran position differs from..."

If a chunk fails: split into ≤400 lines and retry.
