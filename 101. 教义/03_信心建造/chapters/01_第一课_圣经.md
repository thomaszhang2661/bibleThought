# 第一课：圣经 (Lesson 1: The Scriptures)

这本书一直以来都是最畅销的。它已经被翻译成世界上几乎每种语言。你可以在很多家里找到它，甚至在大部分的宾馆梳妆台里。当然了，我们所说的是圣经。

This book has always been the best seller. It has been translated into almost every language in the world. You can find it in many homes, and even in the drawers of most hotel rooms. Of course, we're talking about the Bible.

在这个圣经学习里，我们将进一步地学习圣经，为了让我们看到圣经是比一本普通的书要多的多。为了完成这个任务，我们将需要回答下列问题：

In this Bible study we are going to take a closer look at the Bible — the Scriptures — in order to see that the Bible is much, much more than just a book. To do that, we will need to answer the following questions:

- 什么是圣经？ (What is the Bible?)
- 圣经的主要信息是什么？ (What is the main message of the Bible?)

## 什么是圣经？ (What Is the Bible?)

>

3. 理解谁为圣经信息负责为什么如此重要呢？ (Why is it so crucial for us to understand who is responsible for the message of Scripture?)

有时，我们使用"逐字灵感"这个词语来描述上帝是如何使用先知和使徒来传递圣经信息的。

Sometimes we use the phrase "verbal inspiration" to describe the way God used the prophets and the apostles to convey the message of the Scriptures.

**定义：逐字灵感 (Verbal Inspiration)** ——上帝"把气息送入"先知和使徒的神迹，让他们按照上帝的旨意书写圣经。这被称为逐字灵感，显示了上帝引导他们使用上帝所想要写下的确切词语。

The miracle by which God "breathed into" the prophets and the apostles what he wanted them to write in the Bible. It is called verbal inspiration to show that God guided them to use the exact words he wanted them to write.

人们经常犯的一个错误是臆断圣经包含两部分信息——一部分可以在旧约里找到，一部分可以在新约里找到。旧约比较长，包括39卷书，而新约只有27卷书。旧约书写时间跨度很久，大概从公元前1450年到公元前450年。新约的书写时间则大约为公元后一世纪的后半部分。旧约几乎全部用希伯来文书写，而新约则完全用希腊文书写。（希伯来文及希腊文样例请参见附录I）

One mistake that people often make is assuming that the Scriptures contain TWO messages — one found in the Old Testament and one found in the New Testament. The Old Testament is longer, containing 39 books to the New Testament's 27 books. The Old Testament was written over a longer span of time, from approximately 1450BC to 450BC. The New Testament was written in approximately the last half of the first century. The Old Testament was written almost entirely in Hebrew, the New Testament entirely in Greek.

然而，旧约和新约包含着同样的基本信息。作为复习圣经主要信息的方式，让我们一起来看看来自旧约和新约的经文集合。

## 深入思考 (Taking It Deeper)

A. 同意与否——圣经包括上帝的话语。
   Agree or Disagree — The Bible contains the Word of God.

B. 同意与否——圣经里的每一个词语都是正确的，因为圣经是上帝逐字灵感而著成的。
   Agree or Disagree — Every word of the Bible is true because the Bible is inspired by God.

C. 同意与否——当阅读圣经时，我们必须小心，并理解有些适用于公元后一世纪基督徒的，未必适用于今天的基督徒。
   Agree or Disagree — When reading the Bible, we must be careful to understand that what was true for the Christians of the first century wouldn't necessarily be true for Christians today.

D. 问题——我们说上帝使用逐字灵感来表达他想要先知和使徒所写下的确切信息。我们也说圣经里的书卷最初使用希伯来文或者希腊文所书写的。因此，当我们阅读英文或中文翻译本时，我们应该意识到什么？
   Question — We said that God used verbal inspiration to convey the exact message he wanted through the prophets and apostles. We also said that the books of the Bible were originally written in either Hebrew or Greek. Therefore, what do we need to recognize as we read our English or Chinese translations?

E. 问题——我们说圣经包括律法和福音，我们也学习了这两部分的内容。如果我们只告诉某人关于上帝的律法，而没有提到福音，那会怎么样呢？相反地，如果我们只告诉某人福音，但是没有提到律法呢？
   Question — We said the Bible contains both Law and Gospel, and we looked at what each of those tell us. What would happen if we only told someone about God's Law, and left out the Gospel? What about if we only told someone of the Gospel, but left out the Law?

F. 问题：旧约信徒和新约信徒被拯救的方式是一样的吗？（参看创世记15:6和罗马书4:3。）
   Question: Were Old Testament believers saved the same way New Testament believers are? (Take a look at Genesis 15:6 and Romans 4:3 for help.)
