** COLIN BRYAR** joined Amazon in 1998 – four years after its founding – and spent the next twelve years as part of its senior leadership team as the company grew from a US-only seller of books to a global, multi-dimensional powerhouse and innovator. For two of his years at Amazon, Colin was Chief of Staff to Jeff Bezos, aka ‘Jeff’s shadow’.

![image](Image00011.jpg)

** BILL CARR** joined Amazon in 1999 and spent more than fifteen years at the company. As Vice President of Digital Media, Bill launched and managed the company’s global digital music and video businesses, including Amazon Music, Prime Video, and Amazon Studios.

Colin and Bill are co-founders of Working Backwards LLC, where they coach executives at both large and early-stage companies on how to implement the management practices developed at Amazon.
