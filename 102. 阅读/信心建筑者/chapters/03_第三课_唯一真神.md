# 第三课：唯一真神 (Lesson 3: The One True God)

> 诗篇 14:1 愚顽人心里说："没有神。"
> Psalm 14:1 The fool says in his heart, "There is no God."

---

## 一、上帝存在的知识 (The Knowledge of God's Existence)

人们可以通过观看自然万物或者通过聆听心中良知而得知上帝是存在的。

People can know that there is a God by looking at nature or by listening to their conscience.

### 1. 上帝创造的记录 (The Account of Creation)

圣经在创世记 1:1–2:3 描述了这个世界和宇宙存在的开始。

**创造之星 (The Creation Star)**

| 天数 | Day |
|------|-----|
| 第一天 | The First Day |
| 第二天 | The Second Day |
| 第三天 | The Third Day |
| 第四天 | The Fourth Day |
| 第五天 | The Fifth Day |
| 第六天 | The Sixth Day |
| 第七天 | The Seventh Day |

"创造之星"是用来帮助我们记忆开始六天上帝之创造的。相对的星角是相关联的。比如参看第二天和第五天上帝所造之物。

The "Creation Star" is meant to aid in memorization of the six days of creation. The opposite corners of the star correspond. For example, note what was created on the second and fifth day.

### 2. 从自然认识上帝 (Knowing God Through Nature)

当你观察所有的创造之物时 —— 星星、星球、海洋、动物、昆虫以及人类 —— 你可以看到上帝是必然存在的。

When you look at all of creation — the stars, planets, oceans, animals, insects, and mankind — you can see that there must be a God.

> 使徒行传 14:17 "然而为自己未尝不显出证据来，就如常施恩惠，从天降雨，赏赐丰年，叫你们饮食饱足，满心喜乐。"
> Acts 14:17 "Yet he has not left himself without testimony: He has shown kindness by giving you rain from heaven and crops in their seasons; he provides you with plenty of food and fills your hearts with joy."

> 诗篇 104:24 耶和华啊，你所造的何其多！都是你用智慧造成的；遍地满了你的丰富。
> Psalm 104:24 How many are your works, O LORD! In wisdom you made them all; the earth is full of your creatures.

> 罗马书 1:20 自从造天地以来，神的永能和神性是明明可知的，虽是眼不能见，但藉着所造之物就可以晓得，叫人无可推诿。
> Romans 1:20 For since the creation of the world God's invisible qualities—his eternal power and divine nature—have been clearly seen, being understood from what has been made, so that men are without excuse.

### 3. 从良知认识上帝 (Knowing God Through Conscience)

我们说人们不仅可以从自然那里认识上帝，也可以从自己的良知里认识上帝。

We said that people can not only learn about God from nature, but also from their conscience.

> 罗马书 1:32 他们虽知道神判定行这样事的人是当死的，然而他们不但自己去行，还喜欢别人去行。
> Romans 1:32 They know God's righteous decree that those who do such things deserve death, they not only continue to do these very things but also approve of those who practice them.

> 罗马书 2:14-15 没有律法的外邦人若顺着本性行律法上的事，他们虽然没有律法，自己就是自己的律法。这是显出律法的功用刻在他们心里，他们是非之心同作见证，并且他们的思念互相较量，或以为是，或以为非。
> Romans 2:14-15 Indeed, when Gentiles, who do not have the law, do by nature things required by the law, they are a law for themselves, even though they do not have the law, since they show that the requirements of the law are written on their hearts, their consciences also bearing witness, and their thoughts now accusing, now even defending them.

> 使徒行传 16:29-30 禁卒叫人拿灯来，就跳进去，战战兢兢的俯伏在保罗、西拉面前；又领他们出来，说："二位先生，我当怎样行才可以得救？"
> Acts 16:29-30 The jailer called for lights, rushed in and fell trembling before Paul and Silas. He then brought them out and asked, "Sirs, what must I do to be saved?"

> 哥林多前书 2:9 神为爱他的人所预备的是眼睛未曾看见，耳朵未曾听见，人心也未曾想到的。
> 1 Corinthians 2:9 No eye has seen, no ear has heard, no mind has conceived what God has prepared for those who love him.

> 格林多前书 12:3 若不是被圣灵感动的，也没有能说"耶稣是主"的。
> 1 Corinthians 12:3 No one can say, "Jesus is Lord," except by the Holy Spirit.

### 4. 上帝的自然知识（定义）

**定义：上帝的自然知识** —— 上帝存在的知识，可以通过观察奇妙的创造或者聆听自己的良知所获知。该知识无法解释我们是如何得救的。

**DEFINITION: natural knowledge of God** — The knowledge of God's existence that can be obtained by looking at the wonders of creation or by listening to the conscience. This knowledge cannot reveal how we are saved.

### 5. 从圣经获得完整的知识 (Complete Knowledge Through Scripture)

要想获得真正令人满意的关于上帝的知识，我们不仅需要观察自然或聆听良知。

For a truly satisfying knowledge of God we need to look somewhere other than nature or our conscience.

> 约翰福音 5:39 【耶稣说】，"你们查考圣经，因你们以为内中有永生；给我作见证的就是这经。"
> John 5:39 [Jesus said], "You diligently study the Scriptures because you think that by them you possess eternal life. These are the Scriptures that testify about me."

> 提摩太后书 3:15 并且知道你是从小明白圣经，这圣经能使你因信基督耶稣，有得救的智慧。
> 2 Timothy 3:15 From infancy you have known the holy Scriptures, which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith in Christ Jesus.

## 二、三位一体的上帝 (The Triune God)

我们无法从自然或者从我们的良知中所学到的关于上帝存在的另一方面，则是他是一位"三位一体的上帝"。

Another aspect about God's existence that we cannot learn from nature or from our conscience is that he is a "Triune God."

### 1. 唯一真神 (One True God)

"三位一体"这个词并没有出现在圣经里。这是一个"宗教词汇"——是教会用来合适地描述记载于圣经里关于上帝的真理。

It should be made clear that the term "triune" does not appear in the pages of the Scriptures. It is an "ecclesiastical term" — a word the church uses to conveniently describe a truth about our God that is recorded in Scriptures.

> 提摩太前书 2:5 因为只有一位神，在神和人中间，只有一位中保，乃是降世为人的基督耶稣。
> 1 Timothy 2:5 For there is one God and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus.

> 申命记 6:4 以色列啊，你要听！耶和华我们神是独一的主。
> Deuteronomy 6:4 Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one.

> 罗马书 3:29-30 难道神只作犹太人的神吗？不也是作外邦人的神吗？是的，也作外邦人的神。神既是一位。
> Romans 3:29-30 Is God the God of Jews only? Is he not the God of Gentiles too? Yes, of Gentiles too, since there is only one God.

### 2. 三个位格 (Three Persons)

但是圣经以一种令人惊叹和不可思议的方式来描述上帝。

But the Bible describes God in an amazing and marvelous way.

> 创世记 1:26 神说："我们要...造人..."
> Genesis 1:26 Then God said, "Let us make man..."

> 以赛亚书 6:8 我又听见主的声音说："我可以差遣谁呢？谁肯为我们去呢？"
> Isaiah 6:8 Then I heard the voice of the Lord saying, "Whom shall I send? And who will go for us?"

> 民数记 6:24-26 "愿耶和华赐福给你，保护你。愿耶和华使他的脸光照你，赐恩给你。愿耶和华向你仰脸，赐你平安。"
> Numbers 6:24-26 "The LORD bless you and keep you; the LORD make his face shine upon you and be gracious to you; the LORD turn his face toward you and give you peace."

> 马太福音 28:19 所以，你们要去，使万民作我的门徒，奉父、子、圣灵的名给他们施洗。
> Matthew 28:19 Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.

> 格林多后书 13:14 愿主耶稣基督的恩惠、神【圣父】的慈爱、圣灵的感动，常与你们众人同在！
> 2 Corinthians 13:14 May the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God [i.e.- God the Father], and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit be with you all.

### 3. 三位一体图表 (The Trinity Diagram)

"三位一体真神"这个概念是我们人脑无法理解的，但是我们仍然相信，因为这是上帝之话语的教导。"Tri-"的意思是三，而"-une"的意思是一。

The concept of a "three-in-one" God is impossible for our minds to grasp, yet we believe it because it's what God's Word teaches. "Tri-" means three. And "-une" means one.

**圣父 (THE FATHER)**

- 上帝的名字：约翰福音 20:17 — "我要升上去见我的父，也是你们的父，见我的神，也是你们的神。"
  Divine Names — John 20:17 — "I am returning to my Father and your Father, to my God and your God."
- 上帝的特征：马太福音 5:48 — "所以你们要完全，像你们的天父完全一样。"
  Divine Characteristics — Matthew 5:48 — "Be perfect, therefore, as your heavenly Father is perfect."
- 上帝的行为：马太福音 7:11 — "何况你们在天上的父，岂不更把好东西给求他的人吗？"
  Divine Actions — Matthew 7:11 — "How much more will your Father in heaven give good gifts to those who ask him!"

**圣子 (THE SON)**

- 上帝的名字：罗马书 9:5 — "基督...是在万有之上，永远可称颂的神！"
  Divine Names — Romans 9:5 — "Christ... is God over all, forever praised!"
- 上帝的特征：约翰福音 21:17 — "（对耶稣说）主啊，你是无所不知的。"
  Divine Characteristics — John 21:17 — "(Spoken to Jesus) Lord, you know all things."
- 上帝的行为：约翰福音 5:22 — "父不审判什么人，乃将审判的事全交与子。"
  Divine Actions — John 5:22 — "The Father judges no one, but has entrusted all judgement to the Son."

**圣灵 (THE HOLY SPIRIT)**

- 上帝的名字：哥林多前书 12:6,11 — "神却是一位，在众人里面运行一切的事。这一切都是这位圣灵所运行。"
  Divine Names — 1 Corinthians 12:6,11 — "The same God works all of them in all men. All these are the work of one and the same Spirit."
- 上帝的特征：诗篇 51:11 — "不要丢弃我，使我离开你的面；不要从我收回你的圣灵。"
  Divine Characteristics — Psalm 51:11 — "Do not cast me from your presence or take your Holy Spirit from me."
- 上帝的行为：提多书 3:5 — "他便救了我们，藉着重生的洗和圣灵的更新。"
  Divine Actions — Titus 3:5 — "He saved us through the washing of rebirth and renewal by the Holy Spirit."

**唯一神 (ONE GOD)**

> 申命记 6:4 以色列啊，你要听！耶和华我们神是独一的主。
> Deuteronomy 6:4 Hear, O Israel: The LORD our God, the LORD is one!

### 4. 相信三位一体的途径

> 哥林多前书 2:14 然而，属血气的人不领会神圣灵的事，反倒以为愚拙，并且不能知道，因为这些事惟有属灵的人才能看透。
> 1 Corinthians 2:14 The man without the Spirit does not accept the things that come from the Spirit of God, for they are foolishness to him, and he cannot understand them, because they are spiritually discerned.

> 约伯记 36:26 神为大，我们不能全知。
> Job 36:26 How great is God—beyond our understanding!

> 罗马书 11:33 深哉，神丰富的智慧和知识！他的判断何其难测！他的踪迹何其难寻！
> Romans 11:33 Oh, the depth of the riches of the wisdom and knowledge of God! How unsearchable his judgments, and his paths beyond tracing out!

> 约翰福音 5:23 不尊敬子的，就是不尊敬差子来的父。
> John 5:23 He who does not honor the Son does not honor the Father, who sent him.

> 诗篇 96:5 外邦的神都属虚无；惟独耶和华创造诸天。

## 附录五：进化论理论 (Appendix V: The Theory of Evolution)

很多人认为神创论和进化论的争论是宗教和科学之间、迷信和实验之间的矛盾。该陈述是一个错误的对立。基督徒没有反对科学。他们只是不愿意使自己的信心服从科学。

Many would like to see the creation vs. evolution debate as a conflict between religion and science, superstition and empirical fact. This presents a false antithesis. Christians are not opposed to science. They just do not place their faith in subjection to science.

| 信仰 (BELIEF) | 圣经/神创论 (BIBLE/CREATIONISM) | 科学主义/进化论 (SCIENTISM/EVOLUTION) |
|--------------|-------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| 创世时间 (Time of creation) | 六个24小时整天 (six 24-hour days) | 几十亿年 (billions of years) |
| 创世方法 (Method of creation) | 上帝之话语和命令 (God's Word and decree) | 机会 (chance) |
| 人类存在 (The existence of man) | 上帝特别的创造 (a special creation of God) | 高等动物 (an advanced animal) |
| 人类目的 (Man's purpose) | 服侍和荣耀上帝 (to serve and honor God) | 生存，为自己服务 (to survive, serve self) |
| 人类责任 (Man's responsibility) | 感谢和顺服上帝 (to thank and obey God) | 没有，只有愉悦自己 (none — but to please self) |

于是，矛盾可以归结与此。有两种信仰——神创论和进化论——他们无法调和。不是上帝存在，就是上帝不存在。不是我们是罪人需要救主，就是我们只是需要更进一步进化而得以生存。创世记不是上帝的话语就是神话。

The conflict comes down to this. There are two faiths — creationism and evolutionism — and they cannot be reconciled. In the end, choices must be made. Either there is a God or there is not. Either we are sinners in need of a Savior or we are only occasional beasts who simply need more evolutionary improvement to survive. Either Genesis is God's Word or it is myth.

## 附录六：基督教会的信经 (Appendix VI: The Creeds of the Christian Church)

从基督教会的初期，信徒们便开始通过信仰宣言来声明他们在三位一体上帝中的信心，这便称为信经。该词来源于拉丁词 *credo*，意思是"我相信"。其中三个最有名也最为广泛使用的信经为使徒信经、尼西亚信经，以及阿塔纳修信经。

Almost from the beginning of the Christian church believers have stated their faith in the Triune God through brief statements known as creeds. The word comes from the Latin word *credo*, which means "I believe."

### 使徒信经 (The Apostles' Creed)

我信上帝，全能的父，创造天地的主。
我信耶稣基督，上帝的独生子，我们的主，因圣灵感孕，由童贞女马利亚所生；在本丢彼拉多手下受难，被钉于十字架，受死埋葬；降在阴间，第三天从死人中复活；后升天，坐在全能父上帝的右边；将来必从那里降临，审判活人，死人。
我信圣灵。我信圣而公之教会。我信圣徒相通。我信罪得赦免。我信身体复活。我信永生。阿们。

I believe in God, the Father almighty, maker of heaven and earth.
I believe in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary, suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died, and was buried. He descended into hell. The third day he rose again from the dead. He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty. From there he will come to judge the living and the dead.
I believe in the Holy Spirit, the holy Christian Church, the communion of saints, the forgiveness of sins, the resurrection of the body, and the life everlasting. Amen.

### 尼西亚信经 (The Nicene Creed)

我们信独一上帝，全能的父，创造天地和有形无形之万物的。
我们信独一主，耶稣基督，上帝的独生子，在万世之前为父所生，出于上帝而为上帝，出于光而为光，出于真神而为真神，受生的，不是被造的，与父一性；万物都藉着他受造；为要拯救我们世人，从天降临，因圣灵从童贞女马利亚成了肉身而为人；在本丢彼拉多手下为我们被钉在十字架上，受难，埋葬；照圣经的话第三天复活；升天，坐在父的右边；将来必在荣耀中再临，审判活人、死人；他的国度永无穷尽。
我们信圣灵，赐生命的主；从父、子、而出；同父、子、同样受敬拜、受荣耀；他曾藉着众先知说话。我们信独一圣而公之教会，众使徒所传者。我们承认为赦罪所立的独一洗礼。我们盼望死人复活和来世的永生。阿们。

We believe in one God, the Father, the Almighty, maker of heaven and earth, of all that is, seen and unseen. We believe in one Lord, Jesus Christ, the only Son of God, eternally begotten of the Father, God from God, Light from Light, true God from true God, begotten, not made, of one being with the Father. Through him all things were made. For us men and for our salvation, he came down from heaven, was incarnate of the Holy Spirit and the virgin Mary, and became fully human. For our sake he was crucified under Pontius Pilate. He suffered death and was buried. On the third day he rose again in accordance with the Scriptures. He ascended into heaven and is seated at the right hand of the Father. He will come again in glory to judge the living and the dead, and his kingdom will have no end. We believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the giver of life, who proceeds from the Father and the Son, who in unity with the Father and the Son is worshiped and glorified, who has spoken through the prophets. We believe in one holy Christian and apostolic Church. We acknowledge one baptism for the forgiveness of sins. We look for the resurrection of the dead and the life of the world to come. Amen.

### 阿塔纳修信经 (The Athanasian Creed)

阿塔纳修信经是对三位一体神以及耶稣神性-人性的强有力的辩护，约为五世纪起源于西方，通常在每年的圣三一主日上使用。

The Athanasian Creed is a strong defense of the doctrines of the Trinity and the divinity-humanity of Jesus. The creed seems to have originated in the West around the fifth century. The creed is usually read annually on Trinity Sunday.

凡希望得救的人，首要必须坚持基督徒之真信仰。谁若不完全而纯正地持守这信仰，无疑必永远沉沦。

我们敬拜一上帝在三位格之内，即三位格合一之上帝，不混淆其位格，不分裂其神圣本质。父是个别一位格，子是个别一位格，圣灵也是个别一位格，但父、子、圣灵是一上帝，三者荣耀相同，尊严平等。

在这三位格之间，彼此无先后，无大小，而是，三位格都是互相平等并且永在，故此，一如上述，应在一上帝之内敬拜三位格，应在三位格之内敬拜一上帝。

Whoever wishes to be saved must, above all else, hold to the true Christian faith. Whoever does not keep this faith pure in all points will certainly perish forever.

We worship one God in three persons and three persons in one God, without mixing the persons or dividing the divine being. For each person – the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit – is distinct, but the deity of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit is one, equal in glory and coeternal in majesty.

And within this Trinity none comes before or after; none is greater or inferior, but all three persons are coequal and coeternal, so that in every way, as stated before, all three persons are to be worshiped as one God and one God worshiped as three persons.
