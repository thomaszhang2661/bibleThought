# 第四课：耶稣基督 (Lesson 4: Jesus Christ)

我们所学的每一课和我们将要学习的每一课其实都是围绕着基督的。他的工作是整个上帝之道的聚焦，无论我们讨论上帝话语的哪一部分，都与耶稣拯救我们相关联。

Every lesson that we've looked at and will look at really revolves around Christ. His work is the focus of the entire Word of God, and therefore no matter what portion of God's Word you're discussing, it somehow relates to the way Christ saved us.

**本课核心问题 (Key Questions):**
- 谁是耶稣基督？/ Who is Jesus Christ?
- 耶稣基督的工作是什么？/ What is Jesus Christ's work?

---

## 一、谁是耶稣基督？(Who Is Jesus Christ?)

### 1. 耶稣是真神 (Jesus Is True God)

> 歌罗西书 2:9 因为神本性一切的丰盛都有形有体的居住在基督里面。
> Colossians 2:9 For in Christ all the fullness of the Deity lives in bodily form.

> 罗马书 9:5 列祖就是他们的祖宗，按肉体说，基督也是从他们出来的，他是在万有之上，永远可称颂的神。
> Romans 9:5 Theirs are the patriarchs, and from them is traced the human ancestry of Christ, who is God over all, forever praised!

> 约翰一书 4:9 神差他独生子到世间来，使我们藉着他得生，神爱我们的心在此就显明了。
> 1 John 4:9 This is how God showed his love among us: He sent his one and only Son into the world that we might live through him.

耶稣所行的神迹支持了他是真神的宣告：

The miracles Jesus performed support his claim to be true God:

> 约翰福音 2:7-9 耶稣对用人说："把缸倒满了水。"他们就倒满了，直到缸口。耶稣又说："现在可以舀出来，送给管筵席的。"他们就送了去。管筵席的尝了那水变的酒。
> John 2:7-9 Jesus said to the servants, "Fill the jars with water"; so they filled them to the brim. Then he told them, "Now draw some out and take it to the master of the banquet." They did so, and the master of the banquet tasted the water that had been turned into wine.

> 约翰福音 11:43-44 说了这话，就大声呼叫说："拉撒路出来！"那死人就出来了，手脚裹著布，脸上包著手巾。
> John 11:43-44 When he had said this, Jesus called in a loud voice, "Lazarus, come out!" The dead man came out, his hands and feet wrapped with strips of linen, and a cloth around his face.

> 马可福音 6:41-44 也把那两条鱼分给众人。他们都吃，并且吃饱了。门徒就把碎饼碎鱼收拾起来，装满了十二个篮子。吃饼的男人共有五千。
> Mark 6:41-44 He also divided the two fish among them all. They all ate and were satisfied, and the disciples picked up twelve basketfuls of broken pieces of bread and fish. The number of the men who had eaten was five thousand.

> 马可福音 4:39 耶稣醒了，斥责风，向海说："住了吧！静了吧！"风就止住，大大的平静了。
> Mark 4:39 He got up, rebuked the wind and said to the waves, "Quiet! Be still!" Then the wind died down and it was completely calm.

> 马可福音 1:34 耶稣治好了许多害各样病的人，又赶出许多鬼。
> Mark 1:34 Jesus healed many who had various diseases. He also drove out many demons.

### 2. 耶稣是真人 (Jesus Is True Man)

> 提摩太前书 2:5 因为只有一位神，在神和人中间，只有一位中保，乃是降世为人的基督耶稣。
> 1 Timothy 2:5 For there is one God and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus.

> 罗马书 5:15 若因一人的过犯，众人都死了，何况神的恩典，与那因耶稣基督一人恩典中的赏赐，岂不更加倍的临到众人吗？
> Romans 5:15 If the many died by the trespass of the one man, how much more did God's grace and the gift that came by the grace of the one man, Jesus Christ, overflow to the many!

耶稣的人性也在圣经中清楚可见：

Jesus' humanity is also clearly seen in Scripture:

> 马可福音 11:12 第二天，他们从伯大尼出来，耶稣饿了。
> Mark 11:12 The next day as they were leaving Bethany, Jesus was hungry.

> 约翰福音 19:28 这事以后，耶稣知道各样的事已经成了，为要使经上的话应验，就说："我渴了。"
> John 19:28 Later, knowing that all was now completed, and so that the Scripture would be fulfilled, Jesus said, "I am thirsty."

> 约翰福音 4:6 在那里有雅各井；耶稣因走路困乏，就坐在井旁。那时约有午正。
> John 4:6 Jacob's well was there, and Jesus, tired as he was from the journey, sat down by the well.

> 加拉太书 4:4 神就差遣他的儿子，为女子所生。
> Galatians 4:4 God sent his son, born of a woman.

> 马太福音 26:38 我心里甚是忧伤，几乎要死。
> Matthew 26:38 My soul is overwhelmed with sorrow to the point of death.

### 3. 道成肉身 (The Incarnation)

耶稣的受孕和出生也同样是证据。

The way that Jesus was conceived and born is evidence of this, as well.

> 以赛亚书 7:14 因此，主自己要给你们一个兆头，必有童女怀孕生子，给他起名叫以马内利。（以马内利就是"神与我们同在"的意思。）
> Isaiah 7:14 Therefore the Lord himself will give you a sign: The virgin will be with child and will give birth to a son, and will call him Immanuel. (Immanuel means "God with us.")

> 路加福音 1:30-31, 34, 45 天使对他说："马利亚，不要怕！你在神面前已经蒙恩了。你要怀孕生子，可以给他起名叫耶稣。"马利亚对天使说："我没有出嫁，怎么有这事呢？"天使回答说："圣灵要临到你身上，至高者的能力要荫庇你，因此所要生的圣者必称为神的儿子。"
> Luke 1:30-31, 34, 45 The angel said to her, "Do not be afraid, Mary, you have found favor with God. You will be with child and give birth to a son, and you are to give him the name Jesus. "How will this be," Mary asked the angel, "since I am a virgin?" The angel answered, "The Holy Spirit will come upon you, and the power of the Most High will overshadow you. So the holy one to be born will be called the Son of God."

> 加拉太书 4:4 及至时候满足，神就差遣他的儿子，为女子所生...
> Galatians 4:4 But when the time had fully come, God sent his Son, born of a woman…

**定义：道成肉身** —— 神的儿子呈现为人。指代耶稣的受孕和出生。

**DEFINITION: Incarnation** — The taking on of a human nature by the Son of God. It refers to the conception and birth of Jesus.

### 4. 为何必须是真神真人 (Why He Had to Be Both God and Man)

这对拯救的计划是至关紧要的。为了替我们赎罪，上帝的儿子必须成为人。

This was crucial to the plan of salvation. The Son of God had to become human in order to pay for sins.

> 加拉太书 4:4-5 神就差遣他的儿子，为女子所生，且生在律法以下，要把律法以下的人赎出来。
> Galatians 4:4-5 God sent his Son, born of a woman, born under law, to redeem those under law.

> 罗马书 7:1 弟兄们，我现在对明白律法的人说，你们岂不晓得律法管人是在活着的时候吗？
> Romans 7:1 Do you not know, brothers -- for I am speaking to men who know the law -- that the law has authority over a man only as long as he lives?

> 约翰福音 8:46 【耶稣说】，"你们中间谁能指证我有罪呢？"
> John 8:46 [Jesus said], "Can any of you prove me guilty of sin?"

> 希伯来书 4:15 他也曾凡事受过试探，与我们一样，只是他没有犯罪。
> Hebrews 4:15 We have one who has been tempted in every way, just as we are – yet was without sin.

> 希伯来书 2:14-15 儿女既同有血肉之体，他也照样亲自成了血肉之体，特要藉着死败坏那掌死权的，就是魔鬼，并要释放那些一生因怕死而为奴仆的人。
> Hebrews 2:14-15 Since the children have flesh and blood, he too shared in their humanity so that by his death he might destroy him who holds the power of death — that is, the devil — and free those who all their lives were held in slavery by their fear of death.

> 诗篇 49:7-8 一个也无法赎自己的弟兄，也不能替他将赎价给神，叫他长远活着，不见朽坏。
> Psalm 49:7-8 No man can redeem the life of another or give to God a ransom for him — the ransom for a life is costly.

> 哥林多后书 5:14 原来基督的爱激励我们；因我们想，一人既替众人死。
> 2 Corinthians 5:14 For Christ's love compels us, because we are convinced that one died for all.

> 马可福音 10:45 因为人子来，并不是要受人的服事，乃是要服事人，并且要舍命作多人的赎价。
> Mark 10:45 The Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many.

**耶稣 = 真神 + 真人，通过道成肉身联合在一个人里**

| 真神 (True God) | 真人 (True Man) |
|----------------|----------------|
| 圣经叫他神 (for the Bible calls him God) | 圣经叫他人 (for the Bible calls him man) |
| 他做了只有上帝才能做的事 | 他做了很多人所做的事（比如说吃饭和睡觉） |
| 他有很多神圣的特性（全知全能、圣洁等） | 他有一个身体和灵魂 |

通过**道成肉身**联合在一个人里，所以：
- 他可以在律法之下而出生，他可以死
- 他可以完美地遵守律法，他的牺牲有足够的价值，可以赎所有人的罪

## 二、耶稣基督的工作是什么？(What Is Jesus Christ's Work?)

我们有时用"职务"来指代耶稣曾经做的工作（且仍然在做）。学习圣经时我们可以发现耶稣有三重职务。

We sometimes refer to the jobs that Christ did (and still does) as his "office." When you look at the Scriptures you find that Christ's office is threefold.

> 使徒行传 3:19-20, 22 所以，你们当悔改归正，使你们的罪得以涂抹...主也必差遣所预定给你们的基督（耶稣）降临...摩西曾说："主神要从你们弟兄中间给你们兴起一位先知像我，凡他向你们所说的，你们都要听从..."
> Acts 3:19-20, 22 Repent, then, and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out...he may send the Christ...For Moses said, "The Lord your God will raise up for you a prophet like me from among your own people; you must listen to everything he tells you…"

> 希伯来书 3:1 同蒙天召的圣洁弟兄啊，你们应当思想我们所认为使者、为大祭司的耶稣。
> Hebrews 3:1 Therefore, holy brothers, who share in the heavenly calling, fix your thoughts on Jesus, the apostle and high priest whom we confess.

> 约翰福音 18:37 耶稣回答说，"你说我是王。"
> John 18:37 Jesus answered, "You are right in saying I am a king."

### 1. 先知职分 (The Prophetic Office)

旧约中先知的工作是奉差遣，传讲上帝命令他们说的话。

The job of the Old Testament prophets was to go where God sent them and say what God commanded them.

> 耶利米书 1:7 耶和华对我说："...因为我差遣你到谁那里去，你都要去；我吩咐你说什么话，你都要说。"
> Jeremiah 1:7 But the LORD said to me, "... You must go to everyone I send you to and say whatever I command you."

> 路加福音 8:1 过了不多日，耶稣周游各城各乡传道，宣讲神国的福音。
> Luke 8:1 After this, Jesus traveled about from one town and village to another, proclaiming the good news of the kingdom of God.

> 约翰福音 6:68 西门彼得回答说："主啊，你有永生之道，我们还归从谁呢？"
> John 6:68 Simon Peter answered him, "Lord, to whom shall we go? You have the words of eternal life."

> 路加福音 10:16 【耶稣】又对门徒说："听从你们的就是听从我；弃绝你们的就是弃绝我；弃绝我的就是弃绝那差我来的。"
> Luke 10:16 [Speaking to his disciples Jesus said:] "He who listens to you listens to me; he who rejects you rejects me; but he who rejects me rejects him who sent me."

### 2. 祭司职分 (The Priestly Office)

旧约中祭司的重要工作是献上流血的赎罪祭。

One very important job of the Old Testament priests was to offer atoning sacrifices involving blood.

> 利未记 16:24 【大祭司】出来，把自己的燔祭和百姓的燔祭献上，为自己和百姓赎罪。
> Leviticus 16:24 Then [the high priest] shall come out and sacrifice the burnt offering for himself and the burnt offering for the people, to make atonement for himself and for the people.

> 希伯来书 10:11 凡祭司天天站着事奉神，屡次献上一样的祭物，这祭物永不能除罪。
> Hebrews 10:11 Day after day every priest stands and performs his religious duties; again and again he offers the same sacrifices, which can never take away sins.

> 希伯来书 9:22 若不流血，罪就不得赦免了。
> Hebrews 9:22 Without the shedding of blood there is no forgiveness.

**耶稣在三方面不同于旧约祭司：**

> 希伯来书 7:26-27 像这样圣洁、无邪恶、无玷污、远离罪人、高过诸天的大祭司，原是与我们合宜的。他不像那些大祭司，每日必须先为自己的罪，后为百姓的罪献祭；因为他只一次将自己献上，就把这事成全了。
> Hebrews 7:26-27 Such a high priest meets our need—one who is holy, blameless, pure, set apart from sinners, exalted above the heavens. Unlike the other high priests, he does not need to offer sacrifices day after day, first for his own sins, and then for the sins of the people. He sacrificed for their sins once for all when he offered himself.

> 约翰福音 19:30 耶稣受了那醋，就说："成了！"便低下头，将灵魂交付神了。
> John 19:30 When he had received the drink, Jesus said, "It is finished." With that, he bowed his head and gave up his spirit.

> 以弗所书 5:2 正如基督爱我们，为我们舍了自己，当作馨香的供物和祭物，献与神。
> Ephesians 5:2 Christ loved us and gave himself up for us as a fragrant offering and sacrifice to God.

> 以弗所书 1:7 我们藉这爱子的血得蒙救赎，过犯得以赦免。
> Ephesians 1:7 In him we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of sins.

> 约翰一书 2:2 为我们的罪作了挽回祭，不是单为我们的罪，也是为普天下人的罪。
> 1 John 2:2 He is the atoning sacrifice for our sins, and not only for ours but also for the sins of the whole world.

> 罗马书 4:25 耶稣是为我们的过犯交付了，是为我们称义复活了。
> Romans 4:25 He was delivered over to death for our sins and was raised to life for our justification.

这就是耶稣工作的最重要的一部分，是上帝拯救计划的核心。耶稣为了罪人的利益，成了"替代祭祀"。他替我们生活了一个完美的生命，从这种意义上来看，他是我们的替代。

This is the most important aspect of Christ's work, for it is at the heart of God's plan of salvation. Christ is a "substitutionary sacrifice" on behalf of the sinner. He is a substitute in the sense that he lived a perfect life on our behalf.

**定义：救赎** — 买回来；为让某人自由而付的价钱。

**DEFINITION: Redeem** — to buy back; to pay a price to set someone free.

**基督作为祭司的另一工作——代祷 (Christ's Intercessory Work as Priest)**

> 历代志下 30:27 那时，祭司、利未人起来，为民祝福。他们的声音蒙神垂听，他们的祷告达到天上的圣所。
> 2 Chronicles 30:27 The priests and the Levites stood to bless the people, and God heard them, for their prayer reached heaven, his holy dwelling place.

> 罗马书 8:34 谁能定他们的罪呢？有基督耶稣已经死了，而且从死里复活，现今在神的右边，也替我们祈求。
> Romans 8:34 Who is he that condemns? Christ Jesus, who died -- more than that, who was raised to life — is at the right hand of God and is also interceding for us.

> 约翰一书 2:1 若有人犯罪，在父那里我们有一位中保，就是那义者耶稣基督。
> 1 John 2:1 If anybody does sin, we have one who speaks to the Father in our defense — Jesus Christ, the Righteous One.

### 3. 君王职分 (The Kingly Office)

> 撒母耳记上 9:17 撒母耳看见扫罗的时候，耶和华对他说："看哪，这人就是我对你所说的，他必治理我的民。"
> 1 Samuel 9:17 When Samuel caught sight of Saul, the LORD said to him, "This is the man I spoke to you about; he will govern my people."

> 撒母耳记上 8:20 使我们像列国一样，有王治理我们，统领我们，为我们争战。
> 1 Samuel 8:20 Then we will be like all the other nations, with a king to lead us and to go out before us and fight our battles.

> 哥林多前书 15:56-57 死的毒钩就是罪，罪的权势就是律法。感谢神，使我们藉着我们的主耶稣基督得胜。
> 1 Corinthians 15:56-57 The sting of death is sin, and the power of sin is the law. But thanks be to God! He gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ.

> 希伯来书 2:14 儿女既同有血肉之体，他也照样亲自成了血肉之体，特要藉着死败坏那掌死权的，就是魔鬼。
> Hebrews 2:14 Since the children have flesh and blood, he too shared in their humanity so that by his death he might destroy him who holds the power of death—that is, the devil.

**基督现今与将来的王权 (Christ's Present and Future Kingship)**

> 以弗所书 1:22-23 又将万有服在他【基督】的脚下，使他为教会作万有之首。教会是他的身体，是那充满万有者所充满的。
> Ephesians 1:22-23 God placed all things under his [Christ's] feet and appointed him to be head over everything for the church, which is his body, the fullness of him who fills everything in every way.

> 歌罗西书 2:10 你们在他里面也得了丰盛。他是各样执政掌权者的元首。
> Colossians 2:10 You have been given fullness in Christ, who is the head over every power and authority.

> 罗马书 8:38-39 因为我深信无论是死，是生，是天使，是掌权的，是有能的，是现在的事，是将来的事，是高处的，是低处的，是别的受造之物，都不能叫我们与神的爱隔绝；这爱是在我们的主基督耶稣里的。
> Romans 8:38-39 For I am convinced that neither death nor life, neither angels nor demons, neither the present nor the future, nor any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God that is in Christ Jesus our Lord.

> 使徒行传 1:9-11 说了这话，他们正看的时候，他就被取上升，有一朵云彩把他接去，便看不见他了。...忽然有两个人身穿白衣，站在旁边，说："加利利人哪，你们为什么站著望天呢？这离开你们被接升天的耶稣，你们见他怎样往天上去，他还要怎样来。"
> Acts 1:9-11 After he said this, he was taken up before their very eyes, and a cloud hid him from their sight...suddenly two men dressed in white stood beside them. "Men of Galilee," they said, "why do you stand here looking into the sky? This same Jesus, who has been taken from you into heaven, will come back in the same way you have seen him go into heaven."

> 提摩太后书 4:1 我在神面前，并在将来审判活人死人的基督耶稣面前，凭着他的显现和他的国度。
> 2 Timothy 4:1 In the presence of God and of Christ Jesus, who will judge the living and the dead, and in view of his appearing and his kingdom.

> 路加福音 17:21 神的国就在你们心里。
> Luke 17:21 "The kingdom of God is within you."

> 歌罗西书 3:15 又要叫基督的平安在你们心里作主。
> Colossians 3:15 Let the peace of Christ rule in your hearts.

## 附录七：使徒信经上基督的工作 (Appendix VII: The Work of Christ According to the Apostles' Creed)

学习耶稣已经做了的工作和继续做的工作，其中一种方法是学习使徒信经的第二段。使徒信经非常精彩出色地描述了我们常说的基督之"卑微"和"尊荣"。

One way of studying the work that Christ did and continues to do is by studying the Second Article of the Apostles' Creed. The Apostles' Creed does an excellent job of describing what we call Christ's "humiliation" and his "exaltation."

**定义：卑微** —— 基督神职的第一部分，指当他选择不完全运用他神性的权利以及荣耀，因此他能够以卑微的方式生活以及受死。

**DEFINITION: humiliation** — the first part of Christ's ministry, when he usually chose not to make full use of his heavenly power and glory so that he might live and die in a lowly way.

**定义：尊荣** —— 基督神职的另一部分，即他下到地狱宣告胜利，并他再次完全运用他神性的权利和荣耀。

**DEFINITION: exaltation** — the part of Christ's ministry beginning with his victorious descent into hell when he again made full use of his heavenly power and glory.

### 使徒信经第二段图表 (The Second Article of the Apostles' Creed)

**"我信耶稣基督，上帝的独生子，我们的主"**
*"I believe in Jesus Christ, his only Son, our Lord"*

这段条文承认基督是谁——上帝的独生子，我们的主。

> 马太福音 26:63-64 耶稣却不言语。大祭司对他说："我指着永生神叫你起誓告诉我们，你是神的儿子基督不是？"耶稣对他说："你说的是。"
> Matthew 26:63-64 But Jesus remained silent. The high priest said to him, "I charge you under oath by the living God: Tell us if you are the Christ, the Son of God." "Yes, it is as you say." Jesus replied.

---

**基督之卑微开始 (Christ's Humiliation Begins)**

**"因圣灵成孕，由童贞女马利亚所生"**
*"who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the virgin Mary"*

注意：基督的受孕和出生不等于他卑微的初始。他人性的出生和人性的身体没有限制他权利和荣耀。但是当基督——宇宙之王——来到这个世界上的时候，他情愿不完全使用自己的权利和荣耀。

> 哥林多后书 8:9 你们知道我们主耶稣基督的恩典：他本来富足，却为你们成了贫穷，叫你们因他的贫穷，可以成为富足。
> 2 Corinthians 8:9 For you know the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ, that though he was rich, yet for your sakes he became poor, so that you through his poverty might become rich.

---

**"在本丢比拉多手下受难，被钉在十字架上，受死埋葬"**
*"suffered under Pontius Pilate, was crucified, died, and was buried"*

基督一生谦虚的生命是其卑微的一部分，他在十字架上受难并受死则使其卑微达到顶峰。在十字架上，耶稣忍受了地狱的实质：与天父上帝分离。

> 马太福音 27:46 【耶稣大声喊着说】，"我的神，我的神，你为什么离弃我？"
> Matthew 27:46 [Jesus cried,] "My God, my God, why have you forsaken me?"

---

**基督之尊荣开始 (Christ's Exaltation Begins)**

**"降在阴间"**
*"He descended into hell"*

这可能是第二段中最容易被误解的一句。基督降到地狱标志着他在与撒旦、罪和死亡的战争中取胜。

> 彼得一书 3:18-19 因基督也曾一次为罪受苦，就是义的代替不义的，为要引我们到神面前。按著肉体说，他被治死；按着灵性说，他复活了。他藉这灵曾去传道给那些在监狱里的灵听。
> 1 Peter 3:18-19 For Christ died for sins once for all, the righteous for the unrighteous, to bring you to God. He was put to death in the body but made alive by the Spirit, through whom also he went and preached to the spirits in prison.

> 歌罗西书 2:15 既将一切执政的、掌权的掳来，明显给众人看，就仗着十字架夸胜。
> Colossians 2:15 And having disarmed the powers and authorities, he made a public spectacle of them, triumphing over them by the cross.

---

**"第三天从死人中复活"**
*"The third day he rose again from the dead"*

> 哥林多前书 15:5-8 并且显给矶法看，然后显给十二使徒看；后来一时显给五百多弟兄看，其中一大半到如今还在，却也有已经睡了的。以后显给雅各看，再显给众使徒看，末了也显给我看。
> 1 Corinthians 15:5-8 He appeared to Peter, and then to the Twelve. After that, he appeared to more than five hundred of the brothers at the same time, most of whom are still living, though some have fallen asleep. Then he appeared to James, then to all the apostles, and last of all he appeared to me also.

> 哥林多前书 15:17 基督若没有复活，你们的信便是徒然，你们仍在罪里。
> 1 Corinthians 15:17 If Christ has not been raised, your faith is futile; you are still in your sins.

---

**"后升天"**
*"He ascended into heaven"*

基督升天对我们的信心来说也是至关紧要的。升天表明了基督的救赎工作真正的完成了。基督的升天保证了对我们罪的偿还已经结束并且完成了。天堂是我们的了！

> 约翰福音 14:2-3 【耶稣说，】"在我父的家里有许多住处...我去原是为你们预备地方去。我若去为你们预备了地方，就必再来接你们到我那里去，我在那里，叫你们也在那里。"
> John 14:2-3 [Jesus said,] "In my Father's house are many rooms...I am going there to prepare a place for you. And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come back and take you to be with me that you also may be where I am."

**"坐在全能父上帝的右边"**
*"and is seated at the right hand of God the Father almighty"*

基督为我们的益处而统治一切。他是先知，是祭司，是国王。

**"将来必从那里降临，审判活人，死人"**
*"From there he will come to judge the living and the dead"*

在那时——审判日——没有人能够否认耶稣是主。作为上帝的孩子，我们期盼这天的到来，所以我们能回到我们真正的家——天堂！

> 彼得后书 3:13 但我们照他的应许，盼望新天新地，有义居在其中。
> 2 Peter 3:13 But in keeping with his promise we are looking forward to a new heaven and a new earth, the home of righteousness.
