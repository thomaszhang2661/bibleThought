# 被推动还是被感动？
# *Carried Along or Emotionally Moved? A Semantic and Theological Critique of the Union Version's Translation of* φερόμενοι

## ——和合本「感动」译词的语义分析与神学批判
## *— Semantic Analysis and Theological Critique of the Term* 感动 *in the Chinese Union Version*

---

**摘要**

和合本圣经（1919年）将彼得后书1:21的希腊文φερόμενοι译为「被圣灵感动」，此译法沿用百年，已深入华人教会语言。本文从原文词汇学、翻译链分析、汉语语义史及译本比较四个维度，论证「感动」并非φερόμενοι的精确对应，其情感化语义与原文的外力驱动意涵之间存在显著偏差。委办译本（1852年）将同一经文译为「感於聖神而言之」，保留了被动介词结构，更忠实于原文语义。和合本的改动，经由KJV英文底本「moved」的中介，造成了「φερόμενοι → moved → 感动」的双重语义漂移。这一累积性偏差，客观上为灵恩运动在华语教会的「圣灵感动神学」提供了词汇资源，尽管现有证据不足以支持「灵恩运动影响了翻译」的直接因果主张。本文提出以「被圣灵推动／驱动」为替代译法，并就接受史层面的神学后果加以讨论。

**Abstract**

The Chinese Union Version (CUV, 1919) renders the Greek φερόμενοι in 2 Peter 1:21 as 被圣灵感动 (*carried along / moved by the Holy Spirit*), a translation that has been in continuous use for a century and is deeply embedded in Chinese Christian discourse. This article examines the accuracy of 感动 as a rendering of φερόμενοι from four perspectives: source-text lexicology, translation-chain analysis, the semantic history of Classical Chinese, and a comparative study of Chinese Bible versions. It argues that 感动 is not a precise equivalent of φερόμενοι: the emotionally-charged connotations of 感动 deviate significantly from the original's meaning of externally-driven, passive conveyance. The Delegates' Version (1852) renders the same verse as 感於聖神而言之, preserving a passive prepositional structure that is more faithful to the Greek. The CUV's departure from this rendering — mediated by the English source text's use of "moved" in the KJV — produced a double semantic drift: φερόμενοι → moved → 感动, each step shifting further toward an emotion-centered meaning. This cumulative deviation has, objectively, supplied lexical resources for the Charismatic movement's theology of 圣灵感动 (*the Spirit's prompting*) in Chinese-speaking churches, though the available evidence is insufficient to sustain a direct causal claim that the Charismatic movement influenced the translation itself. The article proposes 被圣灵推动／驱动 (*propelled / driven by the Holy Spirit*) as an alternative rendering, and discusses the theological consequences of the existing translation at the level of reception history.

**关键词**：φερόμενοι；感动；和合本；委办译本；默示论；灵恩运动；翻译链

**Keywords**: φερόμενοι; 感动; Chinese Union Version; Delegates' Version; doctrine of inspiration; Charismatic movement; translation chain

## 一、问题的提出
## I. Statement of the Problem

「被圣灵感动」是华人基督徒日常语言中最熟悉的短语之一。它既出现于圣经文本（彼后1:21；徒11:28等），也大量出现于布道、祷告与灵修文字之中。当一位信徒说「我受到圣灵感动」，意指内心受到某种触动、或感到一种情感性的驱使。这一用法几乎已成为华语教会的神学语言定式。

"Moved by the Holy Spirit" (被圣灵感动) is one of the most familiar phrases in the everyday language of Chinese-speaking Christians. It appears both in biblical texts (2 Pet 1:21; Acts 11:28, etc.) and extensively in sermons, prayers, and devotional writing. When a believer says "I was moved by the Holy Spirit" (我受到圣灵感动), the intended meaning is that their heart was somehow touched, or that they felt an emotionally-charged inner impulse. This usage has become virtually a fixed formula in the theological vocabulary of Chinese-speaking churches.

然而，这一定式的圣经依据——彼后1:21「乃是人被圣灵感动，说出神的话来」——所翻译的希腊原文，其语义与「感动」的日常理解之间，存在着值得严肃审视的张力。

Yet the Greek original underlying this formula — 2 Peter 1:21, "men spoke from God as they were φερόμενοι by the Holy Spirit" — stands in a tension with the everyday understanding of 感动 that deserves serious scrutiny.

本文的问题意识有两个层次：

**其一，翻译精确性问题**：希腊文φερόμενοι（φέρω的被动分词）是否可以精确对应汉语「感动」？两者在语义场上的重叠程度如何？

**其二，神学后果问题**：若「感动」存在系统性的语义偏差，这一偏差在华人教会百年使用史中产生了怎样的神学效应？特别是，它与灵恩运动强调主观经历的「圣灵感动神学」之间，是否存在结构性的亲和关系？

This article addresses two levels of inquiry:

**First, the question of translational accuracy**: Can the Greek φερόμενοι (passive participle of φέρω) be precisely rendered by the Chinese 感动? How far do the two terms overlap in their semantic range?

**Second, the question of theological consequences**: If 感动 carries a systematic semantic deviation, what theological effects has this deviation produced over a century of use in Chinese-speaking churches? In particular, does it bear a structurally affinity to the Charismatic movement's "theology of the Spirit's prompting," with its emphasis on subjective, experiential categories?

## 二、原文分析：φέρω的词义与航海意象
## II. Source-Text Analysis: The Meaning of φέρω and the Nautical Metaphor

### 2.1 φερόμενοι的词汇学分析 / Lexical Analysis of φερόμενοι

彼后1:21的希腊原文为：

The Greek text of 2 Peter 1:21 reads:

> οὐ γὰρ θελήματι ἀνθρώπου ἠνέχθη προφητεία ποτέ, ἀλλὰ ὑπὸ πνεύματος ἁγίου **φερόμενοι** ἐλάλησαν ἀπὸ θεοῦ ἄνθρωποι.

φέρω是希腊语的基础动词，字面意义为「携带、运送、推动、带领」。其被动式φερόμενοι表示「被携带、被驱动、被推送」，动作完全来自外部施力者，受体（先知）处于被动承载状态。

φέρω is a basic Greek verb whose literal meaning is "to carry, convey, drive, lead." Its passive form φερόμενοι means "being carried, being driven, being propelled" — the action originates entirely from an external agent, while the recipient (the prophet) is in a state of passive conveyance.

BDAG（Bauer-Danker希腊文词典）对φέρω在本节中的释义为：「to move something from one place to another, carry, bear」，并特别指出在此语境下有「being impelled」（被驱动）之义。¹

BDAG (the Bauer-Danker Greek lexicon) defines φέρω in this verse as "to move something from one place to another, carry, bear," and specifically notes that the present context carries the sense of "being impelled."¹

### 2.2 航海意象：徒27章的平行用法 / The Nautical Metaphor: Parallel Usage in Acts 27

新约中φέρω的航海意象在使徒行传27章得到最直接的体现。在叙述保罗遇难的航行时，原文两次使用这一词根：

The nautical imagery of φέρω finds its clearest New Testament expression in Acts 27, in the account of Paul's shipwreck voyage. The root appears twice:

- 徒27:15：「船被风抓住，不能迎风，我们就任风刮着走（**ἐπιδόντες φερόμεθα**）」
- 徒27:17：「就降下篷帆，任船漂去（**ἐφέροντο**）」

- Acts 27:15: "The ship was caught by the storm and could not head into the wind; so we gave way to it and were driven along" (**ἐπιδόντες φερόμεθα**)
- Acts 27:17: "they lowered the sea anchor and let the ship be driven along" (**ἐφέροντο**)

这里的φερόμεθα与彼后1:21的φερόμενοι是同一词根的不同变化形式。在航海语境中，其含义极为清晰：船只失去自主方向，**完全被外力（风）推动**，船员无法控制去向。

The form φερόμεθα in Acts 27 and φερόμενοι in 2 Peter 1:21 are different inflections of the same root. In the nautical context, the meaning is unmistakable: the ship loses autonomous direction and is **driven entirely by an external force (the wind)**; the crew cannot control where it goes.

这一意象映射到彼后1:21时，神学含义同样清晰：先知们在说出神的话语时，如同被风推动的船只，**圣灵是外力，先知是被动承载者**。预言的来源与权威完全在于圣灵，而非先知自身的意志或情感。

When this image is mapped onto 2 Peter 1:21, the theological implication is equally clear: when the prophets spoke the words of God, they were like ships driven by the wind — **the Holy Spirit is the external force, and the prophets are passive bearers**. The origin and authority of the prophecy reside entirely in the Spirit, not in the prophets' own will or emotions.

### 2.3 使徒行传2:2的呼应 / The Echo in Acts 2:2

使徒行传2:2描述圣灵在五旬节降临时有「大风吹过（πνοὴ βιαία）」之声，同样使用了风的意象。这并非偶然：彼得后书的作者（无论是彼得本人还是彼得学派）似乎有意以航海风力的意象，来描述圣灵在默示过程中对先知的驱动。这是一个**外力主导、被动承载**的神学模型。

Acts 2:2 describes the coming of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost with the sound of "a violent wind" (πνοὴ βιαία), again employing the wind metaphor. This is not coincidental: the author of 2 Peter (whether Peter himself or the Petrine school) appears to have deliberately used the image of wind-driven nautical force to describe the Spirit's driving of the prophets in the process of inspiration. This is a theological model of **external force and passive conveyance**.

### 2.4 φέρω的语义核心 / The Semantic Core of φέρω

综合以上分析，φερόμενοι在彼后1:21中的语义核心有三点：

Taken together, the semantic core of φερόμενοι in 2 Peter 1:21 comprises three elements:

1. **外力主导**：动作来自圣灵，不来自先知
2. **被动承载**：先知是受体，不是发起者
3. **强制性**：如风推船，带有某种不可抗拒的力量性（参耶20:9「若我说，我不再提耶和华，也不再奉他的名讲论，我心里便有烧着的火……我就不能忍耐」）

4. **External agency**: the action originates from the Spirit, not from the prophets.
5. **Passive conveyance**: the prophets are recipients, not initiators.
6. **Compulsive force**: like wind driving a ship, there is an element of irresistible power (cf. Jer 20:9: "But if I say, 'I will not mention his word or speak anymore in his name,' his word is in my heart like a fire, a fire shut up in my bones… I cannot hold it in").

## 三、翻译链分析：φερόμενοι → moved → 感动
## III. Translation Chain Analysis: φερόμενοι → moved → 感动

### 3.1 和合本的英文底本 / The CUV's English Source Texts

和合本翻译委员会在翻译新约时，以英国修订版（Revised Version, 1885）为主要参考，同时参照钦定本（KJV, 1611/1769）及威斯特科特-霍特希腊文文本（Westcott-Hort, 1881）。²

The CUV translation committee used the English Revised Version (RV, 1885) as its primary reference for the New Testament, alongside the King James Version (KJV, 1611/1769) and the Westcott-Hort Greek text (1881).²

KJV在彼后1:21的译文为：

The KJV renders 2 Peter 1:21 as:

> **holy men of God spake as they were moved by the Holy Ghost**

美国标准本（ASV, 1901，和合本新约完成同期）译为：

The American Standard Version (ASV, 1901, contemporaneous with the completion of the CUV New Testament) renders it:

> **men spake from God, being moved by the Holy Spirit**

两个英文底本均使用「moved」。

Both English source texts use "moved."

### 3.2 「moved」的语义歧义 / The Semantic Ambiguity of "moved"

「moved」在英文中同样具有双重语义：

"Moved" in English carries a double meaning:

**物理义**：被移动、被推动（如船被风moved）——这是φέρω的本义

**Physical sense**: to be moved, driven, propelled (as a ship is moved by wind) — this is the primary sense of φέρω.

**情感义**：被感动、内心受到触动（如「I was moved by the music」）——这是19世纪英语中已相当强烈的引申义

**Emotional sense**: to be emotionally moved, to have one's heart stirred (as in "I was moved by the music") — an extended meaning already well established in nineteenth-century English.

KJV译者选择「moved」时，保留了φέρω的被动物理义，但同时开放了情感义的解读空间。

The KJV translators' choice of "moved" preserved the passive physical sense of φέρω while simultaneously opening interpretive space for the emotional reading.

19世纪末至20世纪初，英语圣经学界已意识到「moved」的歧义问题，开始寻求更精确的替代词：

By the late nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, English biblical scholarship had already recognized the ambiguity of "moved" and began seeking more precise alternatives:

| 英文译本 / English Version | 年份 / Year | 彼后1:21关键词 / Key Term in 2 Pet 1:21 |
|---|---|---|
| KJV | 1611 | moved |
| ASV | 1901 | moved |
| Weymouth NT | 1903 | **impelled** |
| Moffatt NT | 1913 | **carried away** |
| Young's Literal Translation | — | **carried along** |
| ESV | 2001 | **carried along** |
| NIV | 2011 | **carried along** |

这一词汇演变本身就是证据：英语学界逐步以「carried along」（被带动）、「impelled」（被驱使）取代「moved」，正是为了消除情感义的干扰，回归φέρω的物理驱动义。

This lexical evolution is itself evidence: the progressive replacement of "moved" by "carried along" or "impelled" in English scholarship reflects a deliberate effort to eliminate the emotional connotation and recover the physical, driving sense of φέρω.

### 3.3 「感动」的汉语语义 / The Chinese Semantics of 感动

和合本译者将英文「moved」对译为汉语「感动」，这一选择在1906-1919年的翻译语境中有其合理性：「感动」在古汉语中保有外力触发的感应义（见下节），在语感上与「moved」有对应关系。

The CUV translators rendered the English "moved" as 感动, a choice that was reasonable within the 1906–1919 translation context: 感动 in Classical Chinese retained a sense of externally-triggered resonance (discussed in the next section), and there was a natural correspondence of register with "moved."

然而，问题在于**语义漂移的方向性**：

The problem lies in the **directionality of semantic drift**:

- 英文「moved」的情感义：内心受到触动（emotion-centered）
- 汉语「感动」的主导义：情感上被打动、内心受到触发（emotion-centered）

- The emotional sense of English "moved": having one's heart stirred (emotion-centered)
- The dominant sense of Chinese 感动: being emotionally moved, having one's heart touched (emotion-centered)

两次翻译选择均偏向情感义一端，累积效应使最终译文「感动」与φερόμενοι的物理驱动义之间产生了显著距离：

Both translation choices lean toward the emotion-centered pole, and their cumulative effect creates a significant distance between the final rendering 感动 and the physical-driving sense of φερόμενοι:

> **φερόμενοι（航海驱动，外力主导）→ moved（物理义/情感义双重开放）→ 感动（情感义主导）**

> **φερόμενοι (nautical propulsion, external agency) → moved (physical and emotional senses both open) → 感动 (emotional sense dominant)**

这是一个**双重语义漂移**的翻译链，每一步都向情感义方向偏移，最终累积成神学层面的显著偏差。

This is a **double semantic drift** translation chain, each step shifting further toward the emotional sense, ultimately accumulating into a theologically significant deviation.

## 四、汉语语义史：「感动」的古义与演变
## IV. The Semantic History of Chinese 感动: Classical Meaning and Transformation

### 4.1 「感」的字源与本义 / Etymology and Original Meaning of 感

「感」字最早见于金文，字形由「咸」（全、都）与「心」（内心）组成，《说文解字》释为「**动人心也**」。³ 这一字源结构揭示了「感」的基本语义场：外界事物触动内心，是一个**外→内**的方向性过程。

The character 感 appears earliest in bronze inscriptions. It is composed of 咸 (all, complete) and 心 (heart/mind), and the *Shuowen Jiezi* defines it as "to move a person's heart" (动人心也).³ This etymological structure reveals the basic semantic field of 感: something external stirs the inner heart — a directional process moving from **outside → inside**.

### 4.2 《周易》感应论：「感」的哲学化 / The *Yijing* Theory of Resonance: The Philosophical Elaboration of 感

「感」在中国古典哲学中被赋予了宇宙论意涵。《周易·系辞上》云：

In classical Chinese philosophy, 感 was given cosmological significance. The *Xici* commentary of the *Yijing* states:

> 「易无思也，无为也，寂然不动，**感而遂通**天下之故。」（虞翻注：「动也」）

> "The *Yi* has no thought, no action; it is still and motionless, yet **upon being stimulated it penetrates** all situations under heaven." (Yu Fan's commentary: "to move")

咸卦（☱☶）彖辞：

The *Tuan* commentary on the Xian hexagram (Influence, ☱☶) states:

> 「咸，感也……天地感而万物化生，**圣人感人心**而天下和平。」

> "Xian means influence/resonance… Heaven and Earth resonate, and the ten thousand things come into being; the sage **resonates with human hearts**, and all under heaven is at peace."

在《易经》的感应论框架中，「感」是两个事物或力量之间的相互触动与贯通，并非单纯的情感反应。朱熹注：「**感者，因物而动；通者，随感而应**」，强调的是外力触发、内部响应的感应机制。⁴

Within the *Yijing*'s framework of resonance (感应), 感 denotes mutual stimulation and penetration between two things or forces — not a purely emotional reaction. Zhu Xi's commentary reads: "To *feel* (感) is to be moved by an external thing; to *penetrate* (通) is to respond in accordance with that feeling" — emphasizing the mechanism of external triggering and internal response.⁴

这一哲学义项与φέρω的外力驱动义有结构性对应：圣灵（外力）→ 先知（被感应者），恰如《易》中阳刚（外力）→ 阴柔（感应者）的感通模式。

This philosophical sense has a structural correspondence with φέρω's meaning of external driving: Holy Spirit (external force) → prophet (recipient of resonance), just as in the *Yijing*'s pattern of yang force (external) → yin receptivity (respondent).

### 4.3 「感动」在古典文献中的义项演变 / The Evolution of 感动 in Classical Texts

| 时期 / Period | 典型用例 / Typical Example | 语义重心 / Semantic Center |
|---|---|---|
| 战国 / Warring States | 《荀子·乐论》：「足以感动人之善心」 | 外力（音乐）触发内心善性 / External force (music) activating inner virtue |
| 隋代 / Sui | 《隋书·乐志》：「随物感动，播于形气」 | 宇宙感应，外力→内部 / Cosmic resonance, external → internal |
| 宋代 / Song | 朱熹：「因事感动，而恻隐、羞恶之端始觉发露出来」 | 外事触发道德情感 / External events triggering moral emotions |
| 明代 / Ming | 《明史·海瑞传》：「复取读之，日再三，为之感动太息」 | 情感被打动（情感义强化）/ Emotional sense intensified |
| 清代 / Qing | 《红楼梦》：「感动了观音菩萨，夜里来托梦」 | 人影响神（方向反转！）/ Human influences deity (direction reversed!) |

**关键观察**：「感动」的语义演变呈现出两个趋势：

**Key observation**: The semantic evolution of 感动 shows two trajectories:

1. **情感义逐渐强化**：从外力感应义向主观情感义漂移
2. **方向性发生逆转**：《红楼梦》中「感动了观音菩萨」，施动者与受动者的关系已与圣经默示论完全相反——是人影响神，而非神驱动人

3. **Progressive intensification of the emotional sense**: a drift from the resonance meaning toward subjective emotional meaning.
4. **Reversal of directionality**: in the *Dream of the Red Chamber*, "moved Guanyin Bodhisattva" — the relationship between agent and recipient is the exact opposite of the biblical doctrine of inspiration: humans influence the divine, rather than the divine driving humans.

### 4.4 1919年「感动」的语义环境 / The Semantic Environment of 感动 in 1919

和合本出版于1919年，正值文言向白话的转型期（五四运动同年）。此时「感动」的语义环境已相当复杂：

The CUV was published in 1919, at the moment of transition from classical to vernacular Chinese (the same year as the May Fourth Movement). By this point the semantic environment of 感动 was already complex:

- 古典义（外力感应）：在受过文言教育的读者中仍有一定保留
- 情感义（内心被打动）：在白话语境中已成主流

- Classical sense (external resonance): still retained among readers with a classical education.
- Emotional sense (having one's heart moved): already dominant in the vernacular context.

和合本的目标读者主要是普通信众，「感动」的情感义理解在接受层面占据主导。这意味着，即便译者有意借用「感动」的古典感应义，一般读者的理解路径依然偏向情感体验。

The CUV's target audience was primarily ordinary believers, so the emotional reading of 感动 dominated at the reception level. This means that even if the translators intended to exploit the classical resonance sense of 感动, the interpretive path of general readers still inclined toward emotional experience.

## 五、委办译本的比较：「感於聖神」的语义优势
## V. Comparison with the Delegates' Version: The Semantic Advantages of 感於聖神

### 5.1 委办译本彼后1:21原文 / The Delegates' Version Text of 2 Peter 1:21

委办译本（Delegates' Version，新约1852年）⁵ 将彼后1:21译为：

The Delegates' Version (New Testament, 1852)⁵ renders 2 Peter 1:21 as:

> 「以古預言、原非臆說、乃上帝聖人、**感於聖神**而言之」

> "Ancient prophecy was not the product of arbitrary conjecture; rather, holy people of God **spoke as they were resonated upon by the Holy Spirit**"

### 5.2 「感於聖神」的结构分析 / Structural Analysis of 感於聖神

「感於聖神」是一个**被动介词结构**：

感於聖神 is a **passive prepositional construction**:

- **感**：被感应、被触动 / to be resonated upon, to be stirred
- **於**：被……（引出施动者的介词，表被动）/ by… (a preposition introducing the agent, marking the passive)
- **聖神**：圣灵（施动者）/ the Holy Spirit (the agent)

整体语义：**被圣灵所感应/触动**，施动者是圣灵，先知是被动受体。

Overall meaning: **to be acted upon / resonated by the Holy Spirit** — the Holy Spirit is the agent, the prophet is the passive recipient.

### 5.3 两个译本的神学差异 / Theological Differences Between the Two Versions

**委办译本「感於聖神」**：
- 语法上明确标示被动关系（「於」字介词结构）
- 「感」保留古典感应义，不带情感色彩
- 圣灵作为施动者地位清晰

**Delegates' Version 感於聖神**:
- Grammatically marks the passive relationship explicitly (the prepositional structure with 於)
- 感 retains its Classical resonance meaning, without emotional connotation
- The Holy Spirit's status as agent is unambiguous

**和合本「被圣灵感动」**：
- 「被」字虽表被动，但「感动」本身是情感色彩强烈的动词
- 「感动」的现代汉语主导义是内心情感反应
- 读者容易理解为：先知内心受到触动（主观经历），而非被外力推动（客观默示）

**CUV 被圣灵感动**:
- Although 被 marks the passive, 感动 itself is a verb with strong emotional connotations
- The dominant modern Chinese sense of 感动 is an inner emotional reaction
- Readers readily understand it as: the prophet's heart being touched (subjective experience), rather than being driven by an external force (objective inspiration)

这一差异在神学上至关重要：「感动」偏向**主观经历论**的默示观，「感於聖神」偏向**外力驱动论**的默示观。前者容易与灵恩运动的即时感动神学对接，后者更接近改革宗的圣经默示论。

This difference is theologically significant: 感动 tilts toward a **subjectivist-experiential** view of inspiration; 感於聖神 tilts toward an **external-agency-driven** view. The former aligns more readily with the Charismatic movement's theology of immediate prompting; the latter is closer to the Reformed doctrine of biblical inspiration.

## 六、林后13:14的译法问题：koinōnia误译
## VI. A Translation Problem in 2 Cor 13:14: The Mistranslation of koinōnia

### 6.1 经文与译法 / The Text and Its Rendering

林后13:14：

2 Corinthians 13:14:

> 「願主耶穌基督的恩惠、神的慈愛、聖靈的**感動**（交通），常與你們衆人同在。」

> "May the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, the love of God, and the **感動 (交通)** [prompting / fellowship] of the Holy Spirit be with you all."

和合本在此将κοινωνία（koinōnia）译为「感动（交通）」，以括号形式并列，「感动」为主要译词，「交通」为补充。

The CUV renders κοινωνία (koinōnia) here as 感动 (交通), listing the two words in parallel with brackets — 感动 as the primary term, 交通 ("fellowship, communion") as a gloss.

### 6.2 koinōnia的词义 / The Meaning of koinōnia

κοινωνία在新约中出现19次，英文圣经几乎一律译为「fellowship」或「communion」。其核心义为：

κοινωνία appears 19 times in the New Testament and is rendered almost universally as "fellowship" or "communion" in English Bibles. Its core meaning comprises:

- 共同参与（participation）
- 团契、分享（sharing, communion）
- 位格性的相交关系（personal fellowship）

- Participation (共同参与)
- Sharing and communal fellowship (团契、分享)
- Relational, personal communion (位格性的相交关系)

BDAG词典释义：「close association involving mutual interests and sharing, association, communion, fellowship, close relationship」。

BDAG defines it as: "close association involving mutual interests and sharing, association, communion, fellowship, close relationship."

κοινωνία与φέρω完全不同：它描述的是**关系性的参与**，而非动作性的驱动。「圣灵的koinōnia」指圣灵与信徒之间的团契关系，是圣灵的位格性临在与交通，而非一种情感触动的动作。

κοινωνία is entirely different from φέρω: it describes **relational participation**, not active driving. "The koinōnia of the Holy Spirit" refers to the fellowship relationship between the Spirit and believers — the Spirit's personal presence and communion — not an act of emotional stirring.

### 6.3 「感动」的误植 / The Erroneous Use of 感动

将κοινωνία译为「感动」，是将一个关系性名词替换为一个动作性（且带有情感色彩的）名词，属于译义根本性偏差。正确译法应为：「圣灵的**团契**／**交通**／**相交**」（英文：the fellowship of the Holy Spirit）。

Rendering κοινωνία as 感动 substitutes an action-oriented (and emotionally-charged) noun for a relational noun — a fundamental departure from the original meaning. The correct rendering should be: "the 团契 / 交通 / 相交 of the Holy Spirit" (English: the fellowship of the Holy Spirit).

思高本译为「聖神的共融」，新译本译为「圣灵的交流」，均更接近原文。

The Sigao Version renders this as 聖神的共融 ("the communion of the Holy Spirit"), and the CNV as 圣灵的交流 ("the communion/fellowship of the Holy Spirit") — both are closer to the original.

## 九、神学评估：默示论的核心争议
## IX. Theological Assessment: The Central Controversy in the Doctrine of Inspiration

### 9.1 彼后1:21在默示论中的地位 / The Place of 2 Peter 1:21 in the Doctrine of Inspiration

彼后1:21与提后3:16（「圣经都是神所默示的」，θεόπνευστος）并列，是新约圣经自我见证（locus classicus）的两个核心经文。彼得后书的论证结构是：

2 Peter 1:21 stands alongside 2 Timothy 3:16 ("All Scripture is God-breathed," θεόπνευστος) as one of the two *loci classici* of the New Testament's self-testimony regarding inspiration. The argumentative structure of 2 Peter is:

- 19-20节：圣经预言比使徒目击证词更可靠，不可随私意解说
- 21节：原因——预言不出于人意，而是出于圣灵的推动

- Verses 19–20: Scripture's prophecy is more reliable than apostolic eyewitness testimony; it must not be interpreted according to private judgment.
- Verse 21: The reason — prophecy never originated from human will, but from the Spirit's driving.

这里的「推动」（φερόμενοι）指向的是**圣经默示的机制**，而非普通信徒的灵恩经历。将此译为「感动」，使得这一本指圣经默示的独特机制，被普遍化为任何信徒可经历的「感动」状态，模糊了圣经默示的一次性与封闭性。

The "driving" (φερόμενοι) here points to the **mechanism of biblical inspiration**, not to ordinary believers' charismatic experiences. Translating it as 感动 universalizes what was originally a description of the unique mechanism of canonical inspiration into a state of 感动 potentially accessible to any believer, blurring the once-for-all and closed character of biblical inspiration.

### 9.2 改革宗默示论与「感动」的张力 / Reformed Doctrine of Inspiration and the Tension with 感动

改革宗传统（包括和合本主要委员所属的长老会、公理会）对圣经默示持「有机默示论」（Organic Inspiration）或「充分默示论」（Plenary Inspiration）立场：圣灵完全主导预言，但通过先知的人格、文化背景、语言习惯，客观地形成了神的话语。这一过程是**历史性的、一次性完成的**，圣经正典已经封闭。

The Reformed tradition (including the Presbyterian and Congregationalist denominations represented by the major CUV committee members) holds an "Organic Inspiration" or "Plenary Inspiration" view of Scripture: the Holy Spirit fully superintended the production of the prophetic word, doing so through the prophets' personalities, cultural backgrounds, and linguistic habits, while objectively producing the word of God. This process was **historically completed, once for all**, and the biblical canon is closed.

「被圣灵感动」的「感动」语义，倾向于将此过程理解为一种**可复制的主观状态**，与改革宗默示论的客观性立场存在张力。

The semantics of 感动 in 被圣灵感动 tend to construe this process as a **repeatable subjective state**, standing in tension with the objectivist commitments of Reformed inspiration theology.

## 十一、结论
## XI. Conclusion

本文通过原文词汇学、翻译链分析、汉语语义史与译本比较，论证了以下几点：

Through source-text lexicology, translation-chain analysis, Chinese semantic history, and comparative version study, this article has argued the following:

**第一**，φερόμενοι的核心语义是外力驱动的被动承载，如船被风推动，与「感动」的情感触动义存在显著偏差。

**First**: The core meaning of φερόμενοι is passive conveyance under external driving force — like a ship driven by the wind — which deviates significantly from the emotional-stirring sense of 感动.

**第二**，「感动」的出现是双重翻译链（φερόμενοι → moved → 感动）的累积结果，每一步均向情感义漂移，并非灵恩运动直接影响的产物。

**Second**: The appearance of 感动 is the cumulative result of a double translation chain (φερόμενοι → moved → 感动), each step drifting toward the emotional sense. It is not the direct product of Charismatic influence.

**第三**，委办译本（1852）「感於聖神而言之」的被动介词结构，在语法上更忠实于原文，是更优的翻译。和合本放弃这一结构，可能出于文体口语化的考量，但造成了神学语义的损失。

**Third**: The Delegates' Version (1852) rendering 感於聖神而言之, with its passive prepositional structure, is grammatically more faithful to the original and is a superior translation. The CUV's abandonment of this structure, possibly motivated by vernacular stylistic considerations, resulted in a loss of theological semantic precision.

**第四**，「感动」译词与灵恩运动的主观经历神学之间存在「选择性亲和」关系：灵恩神学在华人教会的扩展，在词汇层面获得了和合本「感动」译词的意外支持，尽管这并非译者的本意，也非灵恩运动的刻意操作。

**Fourth**: The rendering 感动 and the Charismatic movement's theology of subjective experience share an "elective affinity": the expansion of Charismatic theology in Chinese-speaking churches has been inadvertently supported, at the lexical level, by the CUV's 感动 rendering — though this was not the translators' intention and was not a deliberate Charismatic strategy.

**第五**，林后13:14将koinōnia译为「感动」属于更严重的误译，将关系性名词替换为动作性情感词，有必要在未来修订中优先纠正。

**Fifth**: The rendering of κοινωνία as 感动 in 2 Corinthians 13:14 constitutes a more serious mistranslation, substituting an action-oriented emotional term for a relational noun. This should be a priority for correction in future revisions.

替代译法「被圣灵推动」在语义准确性上有明显优势，值得汉语圣经修订工作认真考量。百年通行的译词并非不可更易，重要的是译词所承载的神学前设是否忠实于原文的启示论立场。

The alternative rendering 被圣灵推动 ("propelled by the Holy Spirit") has clear advantages in semantic accuracy and merits serious consideration in Chinese Bible revision efforts. A translation term's century-long currency does not place it beyond revision; what matters is whether the theological presuppositions embedded in the rendering are faithful to the revelatory standpoint of the original text.
