# 为何如此多样的原文词汇被统一译为「感动」？
# *Why Were So Many Distinct Source-Text Words Rendered Uniformly as* 感动*?*

## ——五层叠加成因分析
## *— A Five-Layer Analysis of Compounding Causes*

---

这是一个比表面看来更有意思的问题。原因是多层叠加的，不是任何单一解释所能穷尽的。

This is a more interesting question than it first appears. The causes are multiple and compounding — no single explanation suffices.

---

## 第一层：翻译链的路径依赖
## Layer One: Path Dependency in the Translation Chain

旧约的 *tsalach*、*pa'am*、*labash*、*hayah 'al*，英文底本（KJV/RV）对这些词的翻译本身就倾向于使用「moved / came upon / stirred」——都是相对模糊的、可以情感化理解的动词。和合本译者面对的是已经被「平滑处理」过一遍的英文，而非直接面对希伯来原文的词汇多样性。

For Old Testament words such as *tsalach*, *pa'am*, *labash*, and *hayah 'al*, the CUV's English source texts (KJV/RV) already tended to render them with relatively vague, emotionally-amenable verbs: "moved," "came upon," "stirred." The CUV translators were working from English texts that had already smoothed over the original lexical diversity — they were not confronting the Hebrew vocabulary directly.

这意味着原文词汇丰富性在到达汉语译者之前，已经被英文底本部分消解了一次。和合本所继承的不只是英文的词语，更是英文翻译选择已经预设的神学解读框架。

This means that much of the original lexical richness had already been partially effaced before it reached the Chinese translators. What the CUV inherited was not only the English words but also the theological interpretive framework already presupposed by those English translation choices.

---

## 第二层：汉语可用词库的局限
## Layer Two: The Constraints of the Available Chinese Lexicon

1900年代的汉语缺乏现成词汇来对应「圣灵临在于」「圣灵穿上了人」「在灵里的状态」等神学概念。「感动」是当时汉语中语义最接近、最能被读者接受的词。对一个既没有希腊文训练、也没有希伯来文背景的中国普通信徒来说，「感动」确实是一个能理解的词；而「圣灵穿上了他」「圣灵猛然冲上他」则听起来奇怪，甚至令人不安。

The Chinese of the 1900s lacked ready-made vocabulary for theological concepts such as "the Spirit coming to rest upon," "the Spirit clothing a person," or "a state of being in the Spirit." 感动 was the semantically closest, most reader-accessible word available at the time. For a Chinese believer with no training in Greek or Hebrew, 感动 was genuinely comprehensible; whereas "the Spirit clothed him" or "the Spirit rushed violently upon him" would have sounded strange — even disturbing.

译者面临的是一个真实的词库困境：精确的译法（如「圣灵穿上了他」）在文化上没有依托，读者无法将其整合进任何已有的理解框架；而「感动」则能立即激活读者的阅读预期，产生某种（尽管偏移的）意义。在可读性与精确性之间，译者选择了可读性。

Translators faced a genuine lexical dilemma: the accurate rendering (e.g., "the Spirit clothed him") had no cultural anchor — readers could not integrate it into any existing framework of understanding. 感动, by contrast, immediately activated familiar reading expectations, producing some meaning (even if displaced). Faced with a choice between readability and precision, the translators chose readability.

---

## 第三层：功能对等翻译理念的副作用
## Layer Three: The Side Effects of Functional Equivalence as a Translation Philosophy

和合本的翻译目标是官话的可读性——要让没受过教育的信徒听得懂。在这一理念下，译者的直觉是找一个统一的、通俗的、情感上可接受的词，而非保留原文词汇的多样性。这本质上是奈达（Nida）功能对等理论的早期实践——用目标语言读者的感受去对应原文，而不是忠实于原文的词汇区分。

The CUV's translation goal was readability in Mandarin — to make the text comprehensible to uneducated believers. Within this framework, the translators' instinct was to find a unified, colloquial, emotionally acceptable term, rather than to preserve the lexical diversity of the original. This is essentially an early instance of Nida's "functional equivalence" approach in practice — mapping the source text onto the target-language reader's experience rather than faithfully preserving the source's lexical distinctions.

功能对等理念的优势在于可及性：它让圣经真正进入了普通中国人的语言世界。但它的代价同样清晰：当原文用不同的词来区分「主权赋能」「器皿性承载」「先知性临在」「团契性相交」等截然不同的神学概念时，功能对等的统一处理切断了读者通过阅读圣经来区分这些概念的可能性。

The strength of functional equivalence is accessibility: it genuinely brought Scripture into the linguistic world of ordinary Chinese speakers. But the cost is equally clear: when the original uses distinct vocabulary to differentiate such concepts as sovereign empowerment, instrumental conveyance, prophetic presence, and personal fellowship, a uniform functional rendering cuts off readers' ability to distinguish these concepts through reading Scripture itself.

## 第四层：神学意识的缺失
## Layer Four: The Absence of Theological Awareness

这一点最值得深思。译者可能没有意识到，这些不同词汇背后携带着截然不同的神学模型：

This point is the most worth pondering. The translators may not have recognized that these different words carry fundamentally distinct theological models:

- *tsalach* → 主权赋能论 / a theology of sovereign empowerment
- *labash* → 器皿论 / an instrumentality theology (the person as vessel)
- φέρω (被动) → 承载默示论 / a conveyance model of inspiration
- κοινωνία → 位格团契论 / a personal-fellowship pneumatology
- ἁγιασμός → 渐进成圣论 / a progressive-sanctification soteriology

当这些神学概念的区分被「感动」这一个词抹平，读者就失去了通过阅读圣经来辨别这些神学模型的可能性。圣经文本本身所携带的神学多样性，在翻译层面已经被消除。

When the distinctions among these theological concepts are flattened by the single term 感动, readers lose the ability to discern these different theological models through reading Scripture. The theological diversity carried within the biblical text itself is eliminated at the level of translation.

译者不是故意这样做的，但翻译行为本身已经是一种神学决策——无论译者是否意识到这一点。每一次把 *labash*（穿上）等同于 κοινωνία（团契），都是在无声地主张两者在神学上可以互换。这是翻译者不能不承担的神学责任，而它往往在不经意间以最大的影响力发生。

The translators did not do this deliberately; but every act of translation is already a theological decision, whether the translator recognizes it or not. Every time *labash* (clothing) is equated with κοινωνία (fellowship), a silent theological claim is being made that the two are interchangeable. This is a theological responsibility that translators cannot escape — and it tends to exercise its greatest influence precisely when it is least noticed.

## 第五层：「感」字的古典感应义提供了语义掩护
## Layer Five: The Classical Resonance Meaning of 感 Provided Semantic Cover

如主文章第四节分析，「感动」在古典汉语中保有「外力感应」的义项（《易经》的感应论：「天地感而万物化生」），使得这个词在文言/半文言语境中具有一定的语义合法性——它不完全是情感词，也可以表示「被外力触发的感应」。

As analyzed in Section IV of the main article, 感动 in Classical Chinese retains a sense of "resonance triggered by an external force" (the *Yijing*'s theory of resonance: "Heaven and Earth resonate, and the ten thousand things come into being"). This gave the word a degree of semantic legitimacy in the classical or semi-classical register — it was not purely an emotional term, and could signify "resonance activated by an external force."

这层古典语义为译者提供了直觉上的掩护：他们可以在某种程度上合理化「感动」的使用，因为「感」字在古典语境中的确携带外力触发的含义，与圣灵作为外力来源这一神学意象存在某种对应。

This classical semantic layer provided the translators with an intuitive justification: they could reasonably argue that 感, in the classical sense, genuinely carried the meaning of externally-triggered activation, which corresponded in some way to the theological image of the Spirit as an external source of force.

然而，随着汉语白话化的推进（五四运动与和合本几乎同年），「感动」的古典感应义逐渐退隐，情感义成为主导。今天的普通信徒与1919年译者所理解的「感动」之间，已经发生了不可忽视的语义漂移——原本还带有一层「外力感应」含义的词，在百年通行之后，几乎完全被情感化解读所接管。译者所依赖的古典语义掩护，随着语言的演变而失效。

However, as Chinese vernacularization advanced (the May Fourth Movement coinciding almost exactly with the CUV's publication), the classical resonance sense of 感动 gradually receded, leaving the emotional sense dominant. A semantic drift has occurred between what the 1919 translators understood by 感动 and what an ordinary believer today understands — a word that once carried a layer of "external resonance" meaning has, over a century of use, been almost entirely captured by emotional interpretation. The classical semantic cover that the translators relied upon has been eroded by the language's own evolution.

## 综合结论
## Synthesis

综合来看：「感动」的统一使用是**五个因素共同作用**的累积结果：

Taken together, the uniform use of 感动 is the cumulative product of **five compounding factors**:

| 层次 / Layer | 因素 / Factor | 机制 / Mechanism |
|---|---|---|
| 第一层 / One | 翻译链路径依赖 / Translation chain path dependency | 英文底本已平滑处理原文词汇多样性 / English source texts pre-smoothed original lexical diversity |
| 第二层 / Two | 目标语词库局限 / Target-language lexical constraints | 精确译法缺乏文化依托，读者无法接受 / Accurate renderings lacked cultural anchoring and were unacceptable to readers |
| 第三层 / Three | 功能对等理念副作用 / Side effects of functional equivalence | 统一通俗词切断原文神学区分 / A unified colloquial term severed the original's theological distinctions |
| 第四层 / Four | 神学意识不足 / Insufficient theological awareness | 译者未意识到词汇差异携带神学模型差异 / Translators did not recognize that lexical differences carry theological-model differences |
| 第五层 / Five | 古汉语语义掩护失效 / Classical Chinese semantic cover eroding over time | 「感」的感应义随白话化退隐，情感义接管 / The resonance sense of 感 receded as vernacularization advanced, leaving the emotional sense dominant |

没有哪一个因素单独就能解释这一现象，但五者叠加在一起，就形成了这个百年通行却带有系统性偏差的译词选择。

No single factor alone explains the phenomenon. But the five in combination produced this translation choice that has been in general use for a century while carrying a systematic semantic deviation.

这一分析揭示了一个对圣经翻译学具有普遍意义的教训：**翻译层面的词汇决策所产生的神学后果，往往远超译者当时的预期，并随时间的推移而不断放大**。和合本译者在做出「感动」这一选择时，可能并未预见到一百年后它会在灵恩运动的语境中成为「圣灵即时感动神学」的词汇基石。但翻译的历史性后果不以译者的意图为边界。

This analysis reveals a lesson of broader significance for biblical translation studies: **the theological consequences of lexical decisions at the translation level typically far exceed what translators anticipated at the time, and continue to amplify over time**. When the CUV translators made the choice of 感动, they likely did not foresee that a century later it would serve as a lexical cornerstone of the Charismatic theology of immediate Spirit-prompting in Chinese-speaking churches. But the historical consequences of translation are not bounded by the translator's intentions.
